But my non-superhero name is Sam. I am a 27 year old accountant living in Warwickshire. I'm learning to code and I'm aiming to build up a business from the ground up. If you want to learn more about who I am, take a look at my very first blog post!

About

Entrepreneur is not a word that I would have associated with in the past, I didn't grow up like a contestant from the apprentice flogging stuff at the local market. I've never found myself buying and selling junk on eBay hoping to squeeze out a margin. However, today I have an idea for a digital business that I believe could really take off. All I need to do is learn to code, design the product, build that product, develop a brand, find some co-founders, raise some capital, hire some people

This blog is going to document this journey (journey 🤮) as I learn more, develop and design more and hopefully move my idea into a reality. I'm hoping it will help me in developing my coding skills as I test out new techniques on this site, i'm hoping it will help me meet some people interested in similar fields and eventually i'm hoping It will help me build awareness of my new company when I get there.
